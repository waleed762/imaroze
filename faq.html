Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about IMAROZE perfumes, delivery, and more.

How long do IMAROZE perfumes last? +
Our fragrances are designed to provide long-lasting performance, typically lasting 12 to 24 hours depending on skin type and environment. They are formulated with premium EDP concentration for maximum longevity.
Are IMAROZE perfumes suitable for daily use? +
Yes. Our perfumes are carefully formulated for both daily wear and special occasions. The balanced composition works beautifully in offices, casual settings, and formal events.
Do you offer perfumes for both men and women? +
Yes. We offer fragrances for men, women, and unisex preferences. Denve, BrouTe, and Aurex all have a versatile character that works beautifully for any gender.
How should I store my perfume? +
Keep your perfume in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and excessive heat. Avoid storing in bathrooms as humidity can affect the fragrance composition over time.
Are IMAROZE perfumes suitable as gifts? +
Absolutely. Our elegant packaging and premium fragrances make them an excellent gift choice for weddings, Eid, birthdays, anniversaries, and corporate occasions.
Which perfume is best for special occasions? +
Our richer fragrances featuring oud, amber, musk, and woody notes are especially popular for evening wear and special events. Aurex and Denve are particularly recommended for their sophisticated, long-lasting profiles.
Do you deliver across Pakistan? +
Yes, we deliver to all major cities and areas across Pakistan. Orders are typically dispatched within 1-2 business days and delivered within 3-5 working days.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and Bank Transfer. All transactions are secure and private.
