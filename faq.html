Questions

How long do IMAROZE perfumes last? +
Our fragrances are designed to provide long-lasting performance, typically lasting 12 to 24 hours depending on skin type and environment. They are formulated with premium EDP concentration for maximum longevity.
Are IMAROZE perfumes suitable for daily use? +
Yes. Our perfumes are carefully formulated for both daily wear and special occasions. The balanced composition works beautifully in offices, casual settings, and formal events.
Do you offer perfumes for both men and women? +
Yes. Denve, BrouTe, and Aurex all have a versatile character that works beautifully for any gender.
Are IMAROZE perfumes suitable as gifts? +
Absolutely. Our elegant packaging and premium fragrances make them an excellent gift choice for weddings, Eid, birthdays, and corporate occasions.
Do you deliver across Pakistan? +
Yes, we deliver to all major cities and areas across Pakistan. Orders are typically dispatched within 1-2 business days.
