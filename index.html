Our Story

The Art Behind
Every Bottle

Imaroze was born from a singular obsession: the belief that a fragrance should be more than a scent — it should be an emotion, a memory, an identity.

Founded in 2018 by master perfumer Imara Al-Zeid, each composition begins with a journey — to the oud forests of Cambodia, the rose fields of Grasse, the amber deserts of Arabia.

Today, Imaroze stands as a symbol of ultra-luxury craftsmanship, with over 50,000 clients across 35 countries who carry our fragrances as their signature.

Imara Al-Zeid

Founder & Master Perfumer
