Our Heritage

The Art Behind
Every Bottle

Imaroze was founded on a conviction: that a fragrance should transcend scent — it should be identity. Established in Dubai's historic Deira Gold Souk district in 2018, our atelier draws on a thousand-year tradition of Arabic perfumery reinterpreted for the contemporary world.

Each composition undergoes an 18-month development cycle. Our master perfumer sources personally — Cambodian oud from sustainable forests, Taif rose from Saudi Arabia's high-altitude farms, Omani silver frankincense harvested in its brief summer window.

The result: fragrances that are not merely worn, but lived in. Imaroze clients describe finding their "olfactory identity" — a personal signature that becomes inseparable from who they are.

Rania Al-Farsi
Founder & Creative Director — IMAROZE
